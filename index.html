About Us

At Bella Vita, our story is steeped in a passion for Italian culture and culinary traditions. Founded by Jacqueline Lacson, a visionary who has cherished the allure of Italy, our restaurant has been a beloved establishment standing strong for decades. Passed down through generations of the Lacson family, each maintaining a commitment to excellence, our trattoria remains a cherished legacy that invites you to savor the essence of Italian hospitality and cuisine.
